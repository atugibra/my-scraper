      📖 How It Works

      1. Click "Load URLs from Storage" to load your configured leagues
      2. Select leagues you want to process (uncheck ones you don't want)
      3. Click "Process Selected Leagues" to begin
      4. Watch the progress bar - each URL is processed sequentially
      5. Use Pause/Resume if needed (processing stops after current URL)
      6. Process more leagues later - already processed ones are marked with ✅
      7. Click "Download All Processed Data" to generate Excel with ALL leagues
      8. Open Football_Data_All_Leagues.xlsx in your Downloads folder
